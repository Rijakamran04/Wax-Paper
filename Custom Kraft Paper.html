Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between matte and glossy finishes in kraft Paper?

Matte kraft paper has a smooth, non-reflective surface that gives a natural, rustic feel, making it perfect for eco-friendly and understated branding. It's easy to write on and less prone to fingerprints, offering a more subtle, organic look for your packaging.

Glossy kraft paper, with its shiny, reflective finish, enhances colors and gives a polished, premium appearance. It’s more durable and water-resistant, ideal for high-end products or designs that need to stand out.

Will I need to approve my Kraft paper before it's printed?

You got it right! We’ll send you a proof of your Kraft paper for approval before printing begins. This ensures everything meets your expectations and allows for any final adjustments. Your order won’t move to production until you’re completely satisfied!

Can I Update My Order After Payment?

Absolutely! We understand plans can change, and we’re here to help. You can request design changes from the moment we start designing until we send the final design to you for approval. You can also adjust details like sheet size or type before we begin printing. We begin designing within 24 hours of your order, and printing starts right after design approval. Need updates?

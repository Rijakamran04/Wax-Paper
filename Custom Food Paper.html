Frequently Asked Questions

What customization options are available for food paper?

Customization options for food paper include full-color or single-color prints, logos, and custom patterns for branding. Available in various sizes and shapes, with materials like grease-resistant, waxed, and eco-friendly options. Tailor with seasonal designs, custom messaging, or eco-friendly paper to align with sustainability goals, enhancing both functionality and brand visibility.

Will I need to approve my food paper before it's printed?

You got it right! We’ll send you a proof of your food paper for approval before printing begins. This ensures everything meets your expectations and allows for any final adjustments. Your order won’t move to production until you’re completely satisfied!

Can I Update My Order After Payment?

Absolutely! We understand plans can change, and we’re here to help. You can request design changes from the moment we start designing until we send the final design to you for approval. You can also adjust details like sheet size or type before we begin printing.

We begin designing within 24 hours of your order, and printing starts right after design approval. Need updates? Contact us to make your project perfect!
